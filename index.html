What Is Bridgewater Friends Forum?

Bridgewater Friends Forum brings neighbors together to share what we know, love and have lived. No tuition, no prerequisites, no essays and no tests — just ideas worth sharing with friends worth knowing.

🎓  Taught by Neighbors 🤝  Built for Connection ✨  Open to All Residents 💰  Completely Free
